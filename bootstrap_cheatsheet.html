Bootstrap 5 Full Cheatsheet

Daftar class dan komponen Bootstrap 5 yang sering digunakan.

1. Colors

  • .text-primary, .bg-primary
  • .text-secondary, .bg-secondary
  • .text-success, .bg-success
  • .text-danger, .bg-danger
  • .text-warning, .bg-warning
  • .text-info, .bg-info
  • .text-dark, .bg-dark
  • .text-light, .bg-light

2. Spacing

  • Margin: .m-0 sampai .m-5
  • Padding: .p-0 sampai .p-5
  • Arah: .mt-*, .mb-*, .ms-*, .me-*, .mx-*, .my-*
  • .px-*, .py-*

3. Typography

  • .fw-bold, .fw-semibold, .fw-light
  • .fst-italic
  • .text-center, .text-start, .text-end
  • .lh-1, .lh-sm, .lh-lg
  • .text-uppercase, .text-lowercase, .text-capitalize

4. Layout & Grid

  • .container, .container-fluid, .container-sm
  • .row
  • .col, .col-*, .col-sm-*, .col-md-*
  • Gap: .gap-0 sampai .gap-5
  • Display: .d-flex, .d-grid, .d-none
  • Flex: .justify-content-center, .align-items-center

5. Buttons

  • .btn
  • .btn-primary, .btn-secondary, .btn-success
  • .btn-outline-primary
  • .btn-lg, .btn-sm
  • .btn-block (gunakan .w-100 di BS5)

6. Forms

  • .form-control
  • .form-label
  • .form-select
  • .input-group
  • .form-check, .form-check-input, .form-check-label

7. Navbar

  • .navbar
  • .navbar-brand
  • .navbar-nav, .nav-item, .nav-link
  • .navbar-expand-lg
  • .navbar-dark, .navbar-light
  • .bg-dark, .bg-light

8. Cards

  • .card
  • .card-body
  • .card-title
  • .card-text
  • .card-header
  • .card-footer
  • .card-img-top

9. Tables

  • .table
  • .table-striped
  • .table-hover
  • .table-bordered
  • .table-responsive

10. Alerts

  • .alert
  • .alert-primary
  • .alert-success
  • .alert-danger

11. Modal

  • .modal
  • .modal-dialog
  • .modal-content
  • .modal-header
  • .modal-body
  • .modal-footer

12. Offcanvas

  • .offcanvas
  • .offcanvas-start, .offcanvas-end
  • .offcanvas-body

13. Utilities Lain

  • Rounded: .rounded, .rounded-circle, .rounded-pill
  • Shadows: .shadow, .shadow-sm, .shadow-lg
  • Width/Height: .w-100, .h-100, .w-auto
  • Position: .position-relative, .position-absolute, .top-0, .bottom-0
